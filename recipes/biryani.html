Biryani

Description

Biryani is a fragrant rice dish layered with marinated meat, spices, and aromatic basmati rice. It is a staple in South Asian cuisine, known for its rich flavor and colorful presentation.

Ingredients

  • Chicken – 1 kg, cut into pieces
  • Basmati rice – 3 cups
  • Yogurt – 1 cup
  • Onions – 2 large, sliced
  • Tomatoes – 2, chopped
  • Ginger and garlic paste – 1 tbsp each
  • Green chilies – 4, sliced
  • Mint and coriander leaves – ½ cup each
  • Lemon juice – 2 tbsp
  • Red chili, turmeric, coriander powder – 1 tsp each
  • Whole spices (cloves, cardamom, cinnamon, bay leaves) – 1 tsp mixed
  • Saffron (optional) – soaked in warm milk
  • Oil or ghee – ½ cup
  • Salt – to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes, then parboil and set aside.
  2. Heat oil and fry onions until golden brown.
  3. Add chicken, ginger, garlic paste, and spices, and cook until chicken is tender.
  4. Add yogurt, tomatoes, and green chilies; simmer until oil separates.
  5. Layer rice and chicken mixture in a pot, adding mint, coriander, and saffron.
  6. Cover and steam on low heat for 15-20 minutes.
  7. Mix gently before serving with raita or salad.
